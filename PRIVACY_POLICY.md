# Privacy Policy for VocaSheet
**Last updated: November 30, 2025**

This Privacy Policy explains how **VocaSheet** ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles user information.  
VocaSheet is an offline vocabulary study application that allows users to upload their own Excel files to generate quizzes. We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your data remains safe.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**VocaSheet does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**  
The App does not require login, account creation, or any personal information.

The App does **not** collect:
- Name  
- Email  
- Phone number  
- Location data  
- Payment information  
- Device identifiers  
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)  
- Usage analytics  
- Tracking information  

The only data stored is **local to your device**, such as:
- Imported Excel vocabulary files  
- Quiz history  
- Correct/incorrect answers  
- Accuracy statistics  
- Quiz settings (e.g., question count, language direction)

All stored data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use:
- Analytics services  
- Advertising SDKs  
- Tracking technologies  
- Cloud data services  
- External servers of any kind  

All processing is performed locally on the device.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Because VocaSheet does not collect personal information, no user data is stored outside the device.  
Local data such as quiz history and imported files remains fully under your control.

Deleting the App will permanently remove all data stored on the device.

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy

VocaSheet does not collect personal information and is suitable for users of all ages.  
The App complies with general privacy requirements for children’s apps.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Since no personal information is collected or stored, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete beyond what is stored locally on your device.  
Removing or reinstalling the App resets all quiz data and settings.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary to reflect feature changes or legal requirements.  
Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
